Welcome to Tutor Me!

Our tutoring app provides an invaluable platform for both tutors and individuals seeking academic assistance.

Using the structure of a dating app, we promote meaningful interactions between tutors with different skills and students in need of specialised educational help. This unique method promotes a personalised learning environment in which students may locate tutors who specialise in certain topics or areas of study, resulting in a more effective and enjoyable learning experience. .
